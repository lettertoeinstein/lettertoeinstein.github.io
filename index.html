Sigmund Freud letter's key points

Freud writes about history of war

He says  that in the primitive age there was also war among the people but at that time physical power was the means of war. In the Stone Age, because of the invention of the weapons, physical strength was replaced by mental strength because a person having the skill of using the weapon dominated others in the war. In the modern age, due to advanced weapons can dominate others in the war. 

Freud talks about causes of war


He considers there are mainly two causes of war which are material and psychological cause. *Material cause of war is the conflict of desires. 
*Psychological causes concludes that human mind is dominated by two psychological forces which are hadeotic and erotic.

Freud writes about solution of war

Freud believes that there is only temporary solutions of the war as there are no permanent solution.
He says that development of culture using the world wide organization power and fulfilling of the desires are the means of temporary solution of war.
Freud says that such organization should  have military power of all the countries so that it should be used between the war and prevent the battle.

Freud explains why permanent solution of war is impossible.

He explains by using two opposite instincts i.e. Hadeotic and erotic instincts. 
When Erotic force dominates our psychology we become creative and we love to each other. But when Hadeotic force dominates us. We are so much destructive and we desire death everywhere.
Hadeotic force remains in our mind. We always become destructive. Hadeotic force dominates human psychology universally so the problem of war remains in the human world universally, it makes permanent solution of war is impossible.
